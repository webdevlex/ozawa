Liquinox has been specifying and installing Ozawa liquid injection systems since 1986. The Ozawa pump line — the 400 series and 500 series — is the commercial standard for proportional fertilizer injection in drip, micro, sprinkler, pivot, and flood irrigation. We carry the full model range and provide system design support for any scale of operation.
