Liquid Injection Pump Facts:


• Ozawa Liquid Injections Pumps are best used large in commercial applications.

• Chemilizer Pumps are small and are for home owners.

• It is always best to apply fertilizer early in the morning or late in the afternoon.

• Never apply Liquinox Liquid Fertilizer above 90º.

• It's always best to use
• a) Chemilizer Liquid Injection Pump
• b) Ozawa Liquid Injection Pump
• c) Use a sprayer on your hose, this helps to apply the Liquinox Liquid Fertilizer in a even ratio.
