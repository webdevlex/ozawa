Liquinox Start with Vitamin B-1

A transplant solution of growth-promoting substances that includes Vitamin B-1, Alpha Naphthalene Acetic Acid (hormone-like substance), Yucca Extract Base, Chelated Iron and Phosphoric Acid. START is especially formulated to help reduce transplant shock and to stimulate feeder root growth. START is an effective transplant shock and to stimulate feeder root growth. START is an effective transplant solution for use on all types of transplants, including bare root roses, shrubs, bedding plants, seedlings and cuttings. Light frequent applications of START will result in good, healthy root structure and successful planting

  


Liquinox Bloom

A non-nitrogen formula created especially for flowers, fruits and vegetables. Excellent for plants that require greater acid concentration and as a winter food. Contains a wetting agent (yucca extract base) that helps loosen soils and aids in water penetration. Good on all flowers during budding and blooming periods. Helps toughen plants to withstand extreme climates. Available in pints, quarts, gallons and 5 gallon poly pack. Ph = 0.4.

Liquinox Grow

"Encourages All Around Growth" Liquinox GROW is a concentrated all purpose fertilizer especially formulated for use on all types of indoor and outdoor plants. It is an excellent plant food for trees, shrubs, flowers, vegetables, hanging baskets and lawns. The Nitrogen increases foliage and stem growth and helps plants keep vibrant colors. Phosphates encourage root growth, hasten maturity and increase plant hardiness. Potassium helps produce flowers and fruits and builds up plant resistance. GROW is very acidic (low pH - 1.7) and helps neutralize alkaline water and soil. As a general rule apply 1 Tablespoon per gallon of water, once a month
 

Liquinox Iron & Zinc

An essential blend of fully chelated iron and fully chelated zinc designed for correction of chlorosis (yellowing leaves). This fully chelated solution does not contain sulfates. It is readily available to the plants, and may be used in both foliar and soil applications. Available in gallons, quarts and pints.
