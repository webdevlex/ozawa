Ozawa Pump Models & Specifications

Two series — the versatile 400 and the high-capacity 500 — engineered to handle the full range of commercial fertigation demands.

400 Series

150 PSI Max

The 400 series is the most versatile pump in the Ozawa line. Available in single to four-head configurations, it handles injection rates from ounces per hour up to 36 GPH — making it the go-to choice for mid- to large-scale vegetable farms, nurseries, and golf course irrigation systems.

Model Pump Heads Max Flow (GPH) Max Pressure (PSI) Chemicals Injected
401-1 1 36 150 1
402-2 2 36 150 2

404-4 4 36 150 4

400 Series Technical Specifications

  • Positive displacement piston pump with eccentric shaft and roller design
  • Motor: 120V AC or 24V DC (variable speed, computer-controllable)
  • Power: less than 100 watts total at four heads, full output
  • Flow range: ounces per hour up to 36 GPH (adjustable per head)
  • Five adjustment indicators per head in 20% increments
  • Self-priming — operates at any orientation
  • O-ring check valve design with flow-through valve
  • Sealed bearing with self-lubricating materials — zero scheduled maintenance
  • Compatible with fertilizers, acids, soaps, waxes, chlorine, and fish fertilizer

500 Series

180 PSI Max

Built for the most demanding applications, the 500 series reaches 180 PSI and delivers up to 56 GPH in the Direct Adjustable Drive configuration. When you need maximum throughput for large pivot systems, high-pressure lines, or heavy-duty commercial operations, the 500 series is the answer.

500 Series Technical Specifications

  • Heavy-duty positive displacement piston pump
  • Motor: 120V AC or 24V DC with variable speed drive option
  • Maximum discharge pressure: 180 PSI
  • Flow range: up to 56 GPH (model 505½ with Direct Adjustable Drive)
  • Self-priming — can operate at any angle
  • Designed for high-pressure and high-volume commercial systems
  • Compatible with the same chemical range as the 400 series
  • Sealed, self-lubricating bearings — maintenance-free operation

Shared Features Across Both Series

🔌

Dual Motor Options

Every Ozawa model is available with a 120V AC motor for fixed installations or a 24V DC variable-speed motor for solar-powered systems or computer-controlled fertigation programs.

💧

Multi-Chemical Injection

Inject up to four different liquid chemicals simultaneously — fertilizers, pH-adjusting acids, micronutrients, and soil penetrants can all flow through a single pump unit.

📐

Flow-Paced Systems

Pair with the 450 Flow Sensor and 410 Controller for flow-paced injection — the pump output automatically adjusts to match your irrigation flow rate for perfectly consistent ratios.

🔧

Easy Installation

Mounts to 2"–12" aluminum, steel, or PVC pipe using standard saddle clamps. Works with line pressures of 0–150 PSI (400 series) or 0–180 PSI (500 series).
